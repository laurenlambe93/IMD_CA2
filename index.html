A Bit About Me




I am currently a final year student in TU Dublin, Tallaght studying a BSc in Computing with Software Development, and I am due to graduate in 2023. I have always had a keen interest in how things operate and function, especially technology, and when on a website I was always curious as to how a certain piece of functionality worked, so that ignited my pursuit of studying in this field.
I am an eager learner, and I am constantly looking for ways I can upskill and learn new technologies and skills and I undertake Udemy courses in my own time. I would class myself as a great team player as well as being confident working on my own initiative and I pride myself on my communication and time-management skills.
I was a Software Development intern with Workday as part of my 3rd Year work placement program. I spent 7 months working as part of a developed team learning various aspects of IT, and getting real world experience.
